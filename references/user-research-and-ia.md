# 用户研究与信息架构

用户研究、信息架构、用户流程与体验监测工具的完整列表。条目来自 [Awesome-Design-Tools](https://github.com/goabstract/Awesome-Design-Tools)。

## 用户研究 (User Research)
- [Calendly](https://calendly.com/) — schedule meetings without back-and-forth emails.
- [Lookback](https://lookback.io/) — remotely run and record user research sessions.
- [Survey Monkey](https://www.surveymonkey.com/) — online survey tool.
- [Typeform](https://www.typeform.com/) — drag-and-drop forms, surveys, questionnaires.
- [User Interviews](https://www.userinterviews.com/) — recruit research participants by profession/demographics.
- [Handrail](https://handrailux.com/) — end-to-end collaborative user research platform.

## 信息架构 (Information Architecture)
- [Octopus.do](https://octopus.do) — visual sitemap builder with real-time collaboration.
- [WriteMaps](https://writemaps.com/) — create visual sitemaps for website planning.
- [DYNO Mapper](https://dynomapper.com/) — visual sitemaps, content inventory and audit.
- [OptimalSort](https://www.optimalworkshop.com/optimalsort) — card sorting to understand content categorization.
- [Treejack](https://www.optimalworkshop.com/treejack) — test how users move through your proposed sitemap.
- [OmniGraffle](https://www.omnigroup.com/omnigraffle/) — create diagrams and designs (macOS/iOS).

## 用户流程 (User Flow)
- [Whimsical](https://whimsical.co/) — flow charts, wireframes and sticky notes.
- [Overflow](https://overflow.io/) — turn designs into playable user flow diagrams.
- [Lucidchart](https://www.lucidchart.com/) — diagrams, flow charts, sitemaps and more.
- [Draw.io](https://www.draw.io/) — free online diagram software for flowcharts and more.
- [Flowmapp](https://flowmapp.com/) — create sitemaps and user flows online.
- [XMind: ZEN](https://www.xmind.net/zen/) — brainstorming and mind mapping.

## 体验监测 (Experience Monitoring)
- [Hotjar](https://www.hotjar.com/) — see how visitors use your site and collect feedback.
- [FullStory](https://www.fullstory.com/) — capture all customer experience data in one platform.
- [LogRocket](https://www.logrocket.com/) — see what users do to reproduce bugs faster.
- [Google Analytics](https://analytics.google.com/analytics/web/) — measure ROI and track sites and apps.
- [Mixpanel](https://mixpanel.com/) — insights across user-centric data.
- [Amplitude](https://amplitude.com/) — understand users and ship better experiences.
- [Mouseflow](https://mouseflow.com/) — record mouse cursor movements.
