# 创作、灵感与学习

设计灵感、学习、Logo、社媒设计、手绘草图与像素艺术工具的完整列表。条目来自 [Awesome-Design-Tools](https://github.com/goabstract/Awesome-Design-Tools)。

## 设计灵感 (Design Inspiration)
- [Dribbble](https://dribbble.com/) — community for showcasing and discovering artwork.
- [Behance](https://www.behance.net/) — platform to showcase and discover creative work.
- [Mobbin](https://mobbin.design/) — browse mobile design patterns for popular apps.
- [pttrns](https://pttrns.com/) — design patterns for popular mobile apps.
- [Page Flows](https://pageflows.com/) — user flow videos and screenshots for inspiration.
- [Lapa Ninja](https://www.lapa.ninja/) — landing page design inspiration, updated daily.
- [Typewolf](https://www.typewolf.com/) — helps choose the perfect font combinations.

## 设计学习 (Learning Design)
- [Design Better](https://www.designbetter.co/) — best practices and insights from top design teams.
- [Design+Code](https://designcode.io/) — courses about tools and design systems.
- [Interaction Design Foundation](https://www.interaction-design.org/) — well-known for learning UX/UI.
- [Laws of UX](https://lawsofux.com/) — maxims and principles for building user interfaces.
- [Learn UX](https://learnux.io/) — a complete approach to learning UI and UX tools.
- [Figma Training](https://www.figmatraining.com) — a crash course on Figma for UI designers.

## Logo 设计 (Logo Design)
- [Logojoy](https://logojoy.com/) — AI to instantly generate unique logo ideas.
- [Design Evo](https://www.designevo.com/) — vector icons and shapes to design custom logos.
- [Logo Maker](https://www.designhill.com/tools/logo-maker) — generate thousands of logos in minutes.
- [Logo Package Express](http://thelogopackage.com) — export dozens of logo variations in under 5 minutes.
- [Logo Rank](https://brandmark.io/logo-rank/) — see how your logo rates on objective measures.
- [Free Logo Design](https://www.freelogodesign.org/) — create professional logos in seconds.

## 社媒设计 (SMM Design)
- [Canva](https://www.canva.com/) — create designs for work, life and play.
- [Pablo by Buffer](https://pablo.buffer.com/) — design engaging images for social posts.
- [Stencil](https://getstencil.com/) — create social media images easily and quickly.
- [SocialSizes](https://socialsizes.io/) — best image/video sizes for each social platform.
- [Crello](https://crello.com/) — create posts, covers, graphics and posters.

## 手绘草图 (Sketching)
- [Responsive Sketchsheets](https://zurb.com/playground/responsive-sketchsheets) — responsive pre-design templates.
- [Sketchsheets](https://sketchsheets.com/) — free printable device/platform wireframe templates.
- [Sneakpeekit](https://sneakpeekit.com/) — print note-taking grids and device frames.
- [Sketchize](https://sketchize.com/) — printable sketch sheets for your project.

## 像素艺术 (Pixel Art)
- [Aseprite](https://www.aseprite.org/) — animated sprite editor and pixel art tool.
- [Piskel](https://www.piskelapp.com/) — online editor for animated sprites and pixel art.
- [Pyxel Edit](https://pyxeledit.com/) — make tilesets, levels and animations.
- [GraphicsGale](https://graphicsgale.com/us/) — pixel-art features with palette control.
- [Make 8 bit art](https://make8bitart.com/) — open-source in-browser pixel art drawing.
