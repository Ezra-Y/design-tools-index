# 协作、反馈与设计版本控制

团队协作、设计审阅批注与设计文件版本控制工具的完整列表。条目来自 [Awesome-Design-Tools](https://github.com/goabstract/Awesome-Design-Tools)。

## 协作工具 (Collaboration)
- [Notion](https://www.notion.so) — write, plan and collaborate in one tool.
- [Miro](https://www.realtimeboard.com/) — whiteboarding platform for cross-functional collaboration.
- [Airtable](https://airtable.com/) — part spreadsheet, part database, fully flexible.
- [Asana](https://asana.com/) — work management for goals, projects and tasks.
- [Trello](https://trello.com/) — web-based kanban project management.
- [MURAL](https://mural.co/) — think and collaborate visually, anywhere.
- [Milanote](https://milanote.com/) — organize ideas and projects into visual boards.

## 设计反馈 (Design Feedback)
- [Flawless Feedback](https://flawlessapp.io/feedback) — review and annotate iOS apps, share to Jira/Trello.
- [GoVisually](https://govisually.com) — online proofing, design review and approval.
- [Filestage](https://filestage.io/) — review videos, designs and documents with clients.
- [Moqhub](https://moqhub.com) — fast online proofing with image/PDF feedback.
- [zipBoard](https://zipboard.co) — online visual bug tracking and website annotation.

## 设计版本控制 (Design Version Control)
- [Abstract](https://www.abstract.com/) — version, manage and collaborate on Sketch files.
- [Kactus](https://kactus.io/) — design version control without changing your tools.
- [Plant](https://plantapp.io/) — Mac app and Sketch plugin for version control.
- [Versions](https://versions.sympli.io) — visual diff, merge and conflict resolution for designers.
- [Folio](http://folioformac.com/) — a simple Git-based version control for design teams.
