# 3D 建模与增强现实

3D 建模与增强现实（AR）创作工具的完整列表。条目来自 [Awesome-Design-Tools](https://github.com/goabstract/Awesome-Design-Tools)。

## 3D 建模 (3D Modeling)
- [Blender](https://www.blender.org/) — free and open-source 3D creation software.
- [SketchUp](https://www.sketchup.com) — 3D modeling for everyone.
- [Maya](https://www.autodesk.com/products/maya/overview) — animation, environments, VR and character creation.
- [Rhino](https://www.rhino3d.com/) — curve-based 3D modeling for precise surfaces.
- [Vectary](https://www.vectary.com/) — drag-and-drop 3D modeling in browser.
- [Tinkercad](https://www.tinkercad.com) — free, easy app for 3D design and coding.
- [Autodesk Fusion 360](https://www.autodesk.com/products/fusion-360/overview) — integrated CAD, CAM and CAE.

## 增强现实 (Augmented Reality)
- [Spark AR Studio](https://www.sparkar.com) — create AR experiences for Instagram without code.
- [Lens Studio](https://lensstudio.snapchat.com/) — build and publish AR for Snapchat.
- [Unity](https://unity.com/) — build 3D/2D games and deploy across mobile, desktop and VR/AR.
- [Vectary](https://www.vectary.com/) — create 3D and AR for your website.
- [Torch](https://www.torch.app/) — cloud-based 3D design and prototyping for mobile AR.
- [Lightform](https://lightform.com/) — a design tool for projected AR.
