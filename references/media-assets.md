# 媒体素材（免费图库 + 视频 + 音效 + 截图）

免费图库、视频、音效与截图软件工具的完整列表。条目来自 [Awesome-Design-Tools](https://github.com/goabstract/Awesome-Design-Tools)。

## 免费图库 (Stock Photos)
- [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com) — stock photos free to use.
- [Pexels](https://www.pexels.com/) — aggregate of many free stock photo resources.
- [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/) — photos, illustrations, vectors and footage.
- [Burst](https://burst.shopify.com/) — free stock photos for websites and commercial use.
- [StockSnap.io](https://stocksnap.io/) — large selection of free high-resolution photos.
- [Duotone](https://duotone.shapefactory.co/) — free duotone images or make custom ones.
- [UI Faces](https://uifaces.co/) — aggregator of free avatar sources for mockups.

## 免费视频 (Stock Videos)
- [Coverr](https://coverr.co/) — beautiful free stock video footage for homepages.
- [Pexels Videos](https://www.pexels.com/videos/) — free stock footage for any use.
- [Mixkit](https://mixkit.co/) — free HD videos for any project.
- [Videvo](https://www.videvo.net/) — HD clips, motion graphics and free stock footage.
- [Mazwai](http://mazwai.com) — free creative-commons HD video clips.
- [Videezy](https://www.videezy.com/) — free and premium stock footage and motion graphics.

## 音效设计 (Sound Design)
- [Freesound](https://freesound.org/) — collaborative database of CC-licensed sounds.
- [YouTube Audio Library](https://www.youtube.com/audiolibrary/music) — browse and download free music.
- [Bensound](https://www.bensound.com/) — download royalty-free/CC music for free.
- [UI Sounds](https://uisounds.prototypr.io/) — learn sound design for UIs by example.
- [SoundKit](https://soundkit.io/) — a UI sound library for all interface needs.
- [Fugue Music](https://icons8.com/music) — download free background music for videos.

## 截图软件 (Screenshot)
- [CleanShot](https://getcleanshot.com) — superior screen capture with annotation and Quick Access.
- [Snagit](https://www.techsmith.com/screen-capture.html) — capture images/video, create GIFs, annotate, share.
- [Kap](https://getkap.co) — open-source screen recorder exporting GIF/MP4/WebM/APNG.
- [ShareX](https://getsharex.com/) — screen capture, file sharing and productivity.
- [Monosnap](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/monosnap/id540348655?mt=12) — screenshots, annotate and upload to cloud.
- [Giphy Capture](https://giphy.com/apps/giphycapture) — capture screen parts and export as gif/mp4.
