# 图形素材（图标 + 字体 + 插画）

图标、字体、插画素材的完整列表（精选见 `SKILL.md`）。条目来自 [Awesome-Design-Tools](https://github.com/goabstract/Awesome-Design-Tools)。

## 图标
- [Feather Icons](https://feathericons.com/) — icons on a 24x24 grid emphasizing simplicity, consistency and readability.
- [Font Awesome](https://fontawesome.com/) — the web's most popular open-source icon set and toolkit.
- [Material Design Icons](https://materialdesignicons.com/) — free Material Design icons via open-source contributions.
- [Flaticon](https://www.flaticon.com/) — 1.5M+ vector icons in SVG, PSD, PNG, EPS or icon-font format.
- [Iconscout](https://iconscout.com/) — high-quality icons, illustrations and stock photos in one place.
- [Icons8](https://icons8.com/icons) — free iOS, Android and Windows-styled icons.
- [Noun Project](https://thenounproject.com/) — icons for everything.
- [Simple Icons](https://simpleicons.org/) — free SVG icons for popular brands.
- [Ionicons](https://ionicons.com/) — beautifully crafted open-source icons.
- [Iconfinder](https://www.iconfinder.com) — a marketplace for vector SVG icons.
- [SVGRepo](https://www.svgrepo.com/) — 300,000+ SVG vectors and icons.

## 字体
- [Google Fonts](https://fonts.google.com/) — making the web more beautiful and open through great typography.
- [Adobe Fonts](https://fonts.adobe.com/fonts) — thousands of beautiful fonts by Adobe.
- [DaFont](https://www.dafont.com/) — archive of freely downloadable fonts.
- [FontBase](https://fontba.se/) — font management made easy.
- [FontPair](https://fontpair.co/) — helps you pair Google Fonts together.
- [Wakamai Fondue](https://wakamaifondue.com/) — the tool that answers "what can my font do?".
- [Fontface Ninja](https://fontface.ninja/) — browser extension to discover fonts used on any website.
- [Font Squirrel](https://www.fontsquirrel.com/) — download free fonts from wide collections.
- [RightFont](https://rightfontapp.com/) — preview, sync and organize fonts over iCloud/Dropbox.
- [WordMarkIt](https://wordmark.it/) — preview a word with all fonts installed on your computer.

## 插画
- [unDraw](https://undraw.co) — a collection of beautiful SVG images.
- [Blush](https://blush.design/) — create, mix and customize illustrations by artists worldwide.
- [Humaaans](https://www.humaaans.com/) — a free library to mix-and-match illustrations of people.
- [ManyPixels](https://gallery.manypixels.co) — royalty-free illustrations to power up your projects.
- [Open Doodles](https://www.opendoodles.com/) — free CC0 illustrations by Pablo Stanley.
- [Absurd Design](https://absurd.design) — free surrealist illustrations.
- [Blobmaker](https://www.blobmaker.app/) — create vector blob illustrations in the browser.
- [Ouch](https://icons8.com/ouch) — vector illustrations, free for personal and commercial use.
- [IRA Design](https://iradesign.io/) — illustrations from hand-drawn sketch components and gradients.
